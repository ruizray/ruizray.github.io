Message from ComEd
ComEd will never...
Come to your home to request a payment.

ComEd regularly receives reports of scammers posing as ComEd employees. They’ll go to great lengths to try to take your money

Call you to:
  • Ask for your account number
  • Ask for personal information such as your social security number or bank information
  • Ask you to make a direct payment with a prepaid cash card

Avoid being the victim of a scam and learn how to spot energy-related fraud when it may be happening to you. ComEd cares. We’re committed to working together.

Reporting suspicious behavior
If you experience suspicious behavior, please call 1-800-EDISON-1 to report or visit ComEd.com/ScamAlert to learn more.
