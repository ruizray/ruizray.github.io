Utility Billing

Utility bills are sent out through mail/e-mail on a quarterly basis. Bills are due and payable upon receipt. Any bill that remains unpaid for 30 days after the billing date are subject to a 10% penalty.

Online Options

  • If you need assistance, instructions can be found here (PDF)
  • Instructions for setting up automatic EFT Payments can be found here (PDF)

If you do not already have an account, please register here.

Residents can pay for their utility bills by accessing their account.

In Person

  • Deliver payment to the large mailbox located in front of village hall
  • Bring payment directly to the Finance Department

By Phone

  • Call the Finance Department at (630) 226-8430

Parking Tickets

Online options available ONLY FOR PARKING TICKETS. If you were pulled over by police, you can NOT pay for that ticket here.

Online Options

Residents can pay for their parking tickets by accessing the GovPay online payment portal here.

In Person

Please remit your payment within 10 days from the date of the citation payable to: Village of Bolingbrook, 375 W Briarcliff Rd, Bolingbrook Ill 60440

  • Deliver payment to the large mailbox located in front of village hall
  • Bring payment directly to the Finance Department

Water Bill

Online Options

Residents can pay for their water bills by accessing the Illinois American Water online payment portal.

Contact Information

  • If you have questions or need assistance paying your bill, contact the Village of Bolingbrook at (630) 226-8430 or email UBilling@bolingbrook.com.
