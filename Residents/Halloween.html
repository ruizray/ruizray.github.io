Safety Tips

The following Safety Tips are advised for Halloween: Turn on your porch light if you want to be visited by Trick-or-Treaters Have one person hand out candy and wear gloves, if possible. Trick-or-Treaters should only visit houses/apartments of people they know Children should use sidewalks where available and walk, not run, between houses Use caution when crossing streets and only cross at intersections when clear of traffic Costumes should be light and bright so they are clearly visible to motorists and it is suggested that Trick-or-Treaters carry a flashlight Parents should inspect candy and goodies before allowing children to consume Motorists should be vigilant and extra alert during Trick-or-Treat hours Know which friend(s) your child is traveling with if not accompanied by an adult The Bolingbrook Fire Department advises using battery operated lights (not candles) to illuminate Jack-O’lanterns to avoid any contact with flammable materials if the pumpkin gets knocked over The Bolingbrook Police and Fire Departments will have additional units on the streets during the posted hours Mayor Mary and the Village of Bolingbrook Board of Trustees wish all residents a Happy and Safe Halloween! Covid-19

We recognize everyone will make their own health and safety decisions that is best for their children and their home. Please be respectful of your neighbors decisions. If you are NOT interested in having trick-or-treaters come to your home this year, please turn off your porch light and feel free to post our sign. You can download and print the sign or printed copies are available by visiting Mayor Mary's Office at 375 W. Briarcliff Rd.

Live Streaming and Video on Demand

The Bolingbrook Community Television Commission provides coverage of the Village Board meetings, Plan Commission meetings, Valley View 365U School Board meetings and well as other events around the village in High Definition quality and is scheduled for broadcast on the Xfinity Government Access Channel 6 and AT&T U-Verse Channel 99. Many of these events are also streamed LIVE on the official Village of Bolingbrook (@bolingbrook) and the Roger C. Claar Performing Arts Center (@PACbolingbrook) Facebook pages as well as the village website (www.bolingbrook.com/bctvlive). Replays of the LIVE events are also available on demand after the event.

Village Board Meeting Program Schedule:

Live broadcast airs the 2nd & 4th Tuesdays of the month at 7:30pm with replays Monday - Saturday at 1pm.

Plan Commission Meeting Program Schedule:

Live broadcast airs the 3rd Wednesday of the month with replays Monday - Saturday at 10:30am

Valley View 365U School Board Meeting Program Schedule:

Taped broadcast of most the current meeting airs Monday – Saturday at 8:00AM
