Welcome

History is alive and well here! It is our intent to continue to collect and maintain a comprehensive database on Bolingbrook's people, places, structures and events. We work to insure the preservation of historic buildings, document, memorabilia and landmarks in order to make this information available to all residents.

Important Information

Bolingbrook Hisorical Museum 2nd & 4th Saturday’s 1pm to 3pm

The Bolingbrook Historical Museum is located at 444 East Briarcliff. It is open on the 2nd and 4th Saturday of each month from 1pm to 3pm. The museum is open to groups on request. To request a group visit, please call (630)226-8411.

"Images of Bolingbrook"

"Images of Bolingrook" is a local history book written by members of the Historic Preservation Commission. It can be purchased from the Village Finance Department for $21.99.

Donations

We are especially interested in obtaining artifacts and information on the pioneering families of DuPage and Wheatland Townships. We seek donations from anyone cleaning out their attics, garages or homes of relatives. Please do not throw out old snapshots, playbills, yearbooks, tools and utensils. Or if you have something you just can’t part with, how about loaning it to us? We would be glad to display your precious item(s) with a note displaying the message that it is on loan. We will take great care in the handling of your property and place it on display in secured cases at the Bolingbrook Historical Museum.
