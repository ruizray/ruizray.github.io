Mission Statement

The Information Technology Commission supports the dissemination of relevant and timely information on the policies and services civic and community events of the Village of Bolingbrook. We facilitate the effective use and application of technology in our community through programs and services provided to village government, residents and organizations within the boundaries of the Village of Bolingbrook.

Public Meetings

Where:

Village Board Room

When:

1st, 3rd and 5th Thursday of every month Starting at 7:00PM

Upcoming Events

Due to Covid-19 considerations, the Information Technology Commission will be holding its regularly scheduled meetings via video conference. The commission meets at 7:00pm on the first, third, and if there is one, fifth Thursday of the month.

The commission is made up of village residents who advise and support the use of technology to better communicate. We are not part of the village internal Information Technology Department and we do not provide support for day to day computer operations.

In accordance with the Illinois Open Meetings Act, guests with business to bring before the commission are welcome to participate. If you are a resident of Bolingbrook and are interested in attending a meeting, please email your request to infoa@vobitc.org at least 48 hours prior to the meeting date. Include your name, address, and reason for attending. Instructions on how to join the video conference will be emailed back to you.
