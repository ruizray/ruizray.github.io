Mission Statement

The Information Technology Commission supports the dissemination of relevant and timely information on the policies and services civic and community events of the Village of Bolingbrook. We facilitate the effective use and application of technology in our community through programs and services provided to village government, residents and organizations within the boundaries of the Village of Bolingbrook.

Upcoming Events

Hardrive Failure Webinar Tuesday March 23rd 2021 at 7:00 pm

Your hard drive will fail! Learn how to prepare for it on a PC running Windows 10. It's not a matter of whether the hard drive in your PC will crash; it’s when, they all do eventually. Learn how to deal with the inevitable. In this webinar, Jerry Gynac, commissioner with the Village of Bolingbrook Information Technology Commission, will discuss the three “Rs” of Windows 10.

  • Recovery
  • Restore
  • Replace

Learn how to setup these Windows utilities to rescue your data. This event is sponsored by the Fountaindale Public Library. More information will be available in the 2021 spring issue of The Fountain.
