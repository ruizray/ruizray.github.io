About

Established in the early 1980s, BCT got its start as an all-volunteer station that provided programming via Media One’s local cable access channel. As times changed and Media One changed hands to become Comcast, BCT’s volunteers continued to work with the cable operator to broadcast Village Board meetings, youth sports competitions and church services.

Public Meetings

Where

BCT Studio - Lower Level of Village Hall

When

2nd Monday of each month at 7:30pm.

Today

As a Village Commission, BCT provides programming that informs, entertains and preserves the spirit character and diversity of Bolingbrook through the scheduled Village Board meetings, School Board meetings, Plan Commission meetings, free concerts, sports programs, religious services, informational documentaries and specials that highlight what makes the Village unique.

Live Streaming and Video on Demand

The Bolingbrook Community Television Commission provides coverage of the Village Board meetings, Plan Commission meetings, Valley View 365U School Board meetings and well as other events around the village in High Definition quality and is scheduled for broadcast on the Xfinity Government Access Channel 6 and AT&T U-Verse Channel 99. Many of these events are also streamed LIVE on the official Village of Bolingbrook (@bolingbrook) and the Roger C. Claar Performing Arts Center (@PACbolingbrook) Facebook pages as well as the village website (www.bolingbrook.com/bctvlive). Replays of the LIVE events are also available on demand after the event.

Village Board Meeting Program Schedule:

Live broadcast airs the 2nd & 4th Tuesdays of the month at 7:30pm with replays Monday - Saturday at 1pm.

Plan Commission Meeting Program Schedule:

Live broadcast airs the 3rd Wednesday of the month with replays Monday - Saturday at 10:30am

Valley View 365U School Board Meeting Program Schedule:

Taped broadcast of most the current meeting airs Monday – Saturday at 8:00AM
