Trustee Doris has been a Bolingbrook resident since July of 1995. Graduating from Bolingbrook High School in 2007, Troy was involved in both the music and athletic programs. He earned a Track and Field scholarship to the University of Iowa after competing at both the state and national level in high school. Troy moved back to Bolingbrook in 2015 and went on to compete in the 2016 Rio de Janeiro Olympics as well as the 2018 Commonwealth games in Queensland Australia.
