Vax Up Bolingbrook is an initiative by Mayor Mary Alexander-Basta and the Village of Bolingbrook aiming to provide COVID-19 Vaccinations and resources to Bolingbrook residents by partnering with health departments, pharmacies, and other public safety officials.

Any information regarding official Village sponsored vaccination clinics will be updated here:

Our number one priority was to confirm that all our Bolingbrook senior living facilities have been provided the vaccine. We are happy to announce that as of this week, all of their residents were given the opportunity to get vaccinated. Our focus now will shift to senior residents that do not live in these facilities.
After consistent and constant communication with health departments and other providers, Bolingbrook is on track to host vaccination clinics. We are still waiting to secure a supply of vaccine. We anticipate the Village to host the first community vaccination clinic in the coming weeks.

*By filling out this form, you are NOT scheduling an appointment. You are pre-registering for when vaccines become available*

Currently, you are eligible to receive a vaccine if you are a Bolingbrook resident* and meet any of the following criteria:
  • Age 65 years and older
  • Qualify in the 1A vaccination group or 1B vaccination group

*You will be asked to provide a state issued ID or utility bill as proof of residence.
