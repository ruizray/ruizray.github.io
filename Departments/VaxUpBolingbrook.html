Vax Up Bolingbrook is an initiative by Mayor Mary Alexander-Basta and the Village of Bolingbrook aiming to provide COVID-19 Vaccinations and resources to Bolingbrook residents by partnering with health departments, pharmacies, and other public safety officials.

Any information regarding official Village sponsored vaccination clinics will be updated here.

Thanks to our partnership with the Will County Health Department, Bolingbrook is pleased to announce our 3rd Community Vaccination Clinic.

This clinic will be held Monday, April 12 – Wednesday, April 14, 2021.

We have been given a supply of the Moderna Vaccine.

As eligibility opens up, you may receive your first dose April 12 – April 14 if you meet the following qualifications:

  • 18 years old or older
  • Live in Will County
  • Are available to receive your 2nd dose at the same location May 10 – May 12

Interested residents should pre-register by filling out the form below. Appointment scheduling links will be sent to those who pre-register and live in Will County. Registration will stay open as long as vaccine supply is available.

*By filling out this form, you are NOT scheduling an appointment. You are pre-registering for when vaccines become available*

Please continue to check availability through the health departments or pharmacies and take the first available appointment. Click HERE for Will County partner locations.
